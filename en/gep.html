Centralized Registrations

Physical Store Mapping

Includes floor plans and mapping of promotional spaces, allowing precise visualization of space occupancy and availability in each store.

ERP Integration

Synchronizes information with ERP systems, ensuring data consistency and eliminating the need for manual updates, facilitating workflow.

Automated Alerts

Sends alerts about important events and deadlines, such as contract expirations, new negotiations, or renegotiations, ensuring timely actions.
